How Our Tournament Platform Works

1

Find a Tournament

Browse our upcoming tournaments and choose one that matches your skill level and schedule.

2

Register

Click the register button and provide your Free Fire ID and contact information.

3

Get Ready

You'll receive tournament details via email and WhatsApp before the event starts.

4

Play & Compete

Join the custom room at the scheduled time and compete against other players.

5

Report Results

Submit your match results through our system or via WhatsApp for verification.

6

Claim Prizes

Winners will receive their prizes directly in their Free Fire account within 24 hours.
