About Free Fire Tournaments

Our Mission

We're passionate about creating a competitive platform for Free Fire players to showcase their skills, connect with other gamers, and win amazing prizes. Our goal is to make tournament organization and participation accessible to everyone.

How We Started

Founded in 2024 by a group of Free Fire enthusiasts, we noticed a lack of organized tournaments for casual and competitive players alike. We built this platform to fill that gap and create a thriving Free Fire community.
